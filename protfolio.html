About Me

My name is Steven Bender, I was born in the year 1990 in the town of Fargo, North Dakota. naturally being from the north I kind of like winter including watching and playing hockey. I started playing hockey when I was just a wee lad. I really enjoy science fiction and fantacy, especially Star Wars. I have two boys currently and they take a lot of my time. One of my favorite things to do at the end of the day is to just sit and watch a kiddy tv show with them.
